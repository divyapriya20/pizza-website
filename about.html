About dibhani's pizzaria

At the core of Dibhani's Pizzaria is a simple philosophy: great pizza comes from great ingredients and great care. History of our restaurant

Nestled in hyderabad, Dibhani's Pizzeria blends Italian tradition with Indian flair. Founded by dibhani, a culinary maestro with a passion for fusion, each pizza tells a tale of spice-laden adventures. From butter chicken to paneer tikka, every slice is a symphony of flavors, inviting diners on a tantalizing journey.
